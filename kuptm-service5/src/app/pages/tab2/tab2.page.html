FAQ Are the services free?

Most of the services provided are free except for the printing service. The printing service requires students to pay a little sum of money after completion of service. The payment for printing service can be made at the person in charge in KOMASA. The rest of the services are absolutely free.

What happen after I have completed my booking session?

The booking information is kept in our database and student can pick up the booked item or venue.

Are the pass year exam papers legit?

Yes, all the exam papers in this app are legit and we got them from KUPTM staff.

How do I contact the admin of this app?

Student can contact the admin by simply going to 'Setting' and tab 'Contact'. Student can send feedback, complaint or any suggestion to improve the application.
